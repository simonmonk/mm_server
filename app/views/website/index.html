Founded in 2013, Monk Makes Ltd designs and manufacturers a wide range of electronics kits and circuit boards from its base in the North West of England.

Nearly all of our products are manufacturerd or assembled in the UK using our PCB assembly facility and our local workforce.

As well as supporting educators, our products are also popular with electronics makers and enthusiasts.

We are not a retail outlet, we concentrate on out strength in design and manufacturing and so, if you have come here looking to buy a specific product, then please click HERE to find a product information for each of our products. Each product page has direct links to the BUY page from our retailers who stock that product.
